Frequently Asked Questions

What should I look for when buying sleep supplements?

Check the ingredient label first — dose, form, and third-party testing matter more than marketing claims. Look for transparent labeling, NSF or Informed Sport certification when possible, and reviews from real users not sponsored athletes.

Are premium sleep supplements worth the extra cost?

Sometimes — with supplements the price-quality correlation is real but weak. A mid-range product with clean ingredients often outperforms an expensive one with proprietary blends that hide underdosed actives. Read labels, not price tags.

What are common mistakes when buying sleep supplements?

Expecting supplements to replace diet and training. They're called supplements for a reason. Also watch out for proprietary blends — they legally hide ingredient doses behind a single blend weight.

How long should quality sleep supplements last?

Check the expiration date and store properly — most supplements are good 1-2 years from manufacture. Once opened, some degrade faster if exposed to heat and moisture. Keep them in a cool, dry place.

Why buy sleep supplements on Amazon?

Amazon is usually the most convenient option — competitive pricing, fast Prime shipping, and easy returns if something's wrong. That said, always check the manufacturer's site for bundle deals, extended warranties, or direct pricing.
